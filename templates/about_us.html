About Gregbuk Intl Company Ltd

A family-owned Nigerian corporate services company delivering practical, reliable support across operations, compliance, and project execution.

Company Introduction

Gregbuk Intl Company Ltd was established in 2004 and incorporated in Lagos, Nigeria. We are built on family-led leadership, operational discipline, and long-term service reliability for institutions and businesses.

Our registered details: Registration Number 1057410, registered on 10 Aug 2012, and current status Active.

Mission

To deliver dependable, high-quality, and practical service solutions that help organizations execute with confidence.

Vision

To be a trusted reference point for corporate services in Lagos and beyond, known for integrity, consistency, and value-driven outcomes.

Background & Growth

From foundational print and supply support, Gregbuk expanded into digital ID printing, machine sales and distribution, pharmaceutical distribution, engineering consultancy, travel agency support, and CAC/trademark/commission services. This growth reflects our commitment to solving real client challenges with structured delivery.
