Company Profile

About Gregbuk Intl Company Ltd

Gregbuk Intl Company Ltd is a family-owned company established in 2004, incorporated in Lagos, Nigeria, and committed to practical multi-service delivery.

Company Overview

Gregbuk Intl Company Ltd serves businesses, institutions, and government-related operations through dependable service execution across printing and supplies, digital identity solutions, distribution, consultancy, and corporate support services.

Our approach combines business discipline, industry experience, and responsive client support to deliver clear outcomes and lasting partnerships.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, practical, and high-quality corporate solutions that help organizations operate efficiently and grow sustainably.

Our Vision

To be a trusted Nigerian multi-service company recognized for service excellence, integrity, and long-term value delivery.

Background & History

Since 2004, Gregbuk Intl Company Ltd has evolved from specialized print support into a broader corporate service company. Built on family ownership and operational consistency, we continue to expand responsibly while maintaining client trust.
