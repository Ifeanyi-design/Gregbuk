About Gregbuk Intl Company Ltd

Gregbuk Intl Company Ltd is a family-owned company established in 2004, incorporated in Lagos, Nigeria, and currently active with Registration Number 1057410.

We provide multi-service corporate support across contracts, printing, digital identity systems, machine sales, distribution, consultancy, travel support, and business compliance services.
